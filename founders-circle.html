EQLabs Launches Founders Circle to Build the Future of Open, Agentic AI

San Francisco, CA — October 11, 2025 — EQLabs, an open research and product company advancing agentic AI systems, today announced the launch of its Founders Circle—an invite-only community of builders, researchers, and backers who want to co-create the next generation of open AI infrastructure. EQLabs is currently pre-seed and actively welcoming early believers to join and invest.

EQLabs has been exploring mixture-of-agents architectures, low-latency inference, and practical workflows for real-world AI copilots. The Founders Circle gives committed contributors a hands-on way to shape the roadmap from day one.

Why join the Founders Circle

  • Direct product influence: Priority access to private builds, research notes, and roadmap reviews.
  • Early technical edge: Experimental features, performance benchmarks, and integration support.
  • Community of doers: Collaborate with kindred spirits who ship fast and think long-term.
  • Founding recognition: Early member credits and recognition across launch assets.

We’re pre-seed — ways to invest

  • Angel/Pre-seed checks: We’re open to standard SAFE checks; intros welcome.
  • Strategic partnerships: Credits, data, distribution, and co-dev opportunities.
  • GPU donations (fast impact): If you’re upgrading, donate hardware that’s 1–2 generations behind your current GPUs. We’ll put them to work in our research cluster and publicly acknowledge contributors.

We can accept hardware that’s 1–2 generations behind current flagship cards; we’ll verify compatibility and provide documentation for acknowledgment.

What we’re building

  • Agentic AI: Practical, composable agents that can plan, verify, and act safely.
  • Open-first: Transparent research, reproducible results, and community-driven progress.
  • Performance + pragmatism: Real-world latencies, reliable tool use, and measurable outcomes.

A note from the team

“We believe the next wave of AI is about coordination—multiple specialized models working together like a team. If that resonates, we want you in the room,” said the EQLabs founders.

How to get involved

  • Join the Founders Circle: Tell us about your background and what you want to build.
  • Invest or donate GPUs: We’ll coordinate pickup/shipping for eligible hardware and share utilization stats once online.
  • Collaborate: Bring a problem set or dataset—let’s prototype an agentic workflow together.
