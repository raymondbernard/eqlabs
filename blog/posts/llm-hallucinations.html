Reducing LLM Hallucinations: A Deep Dive into Reflection LLM and Vector Stores

September 8, 2024

Large Language Models are powerful, but prone to hallucinations — confident statements that are not grounded in facts. This post explores using a Reflection LLM loop combined with a vector store of verified references to reduce hallucinations while preserving fluency.

We outline a practical pipeline: retrieval of human‑verified context, initial generation, reflection and self‑critique, and a final grounded answer. The approach aligns with our EQUATOR principles: deterministic checks for correctness, completeness, and groundedness.
